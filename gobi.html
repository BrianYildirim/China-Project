Gobi Desert

The Gobi Desert is a desert in Central Asia, near Mongolia and China. It is the 5th largest desert in the world, as it is about 1,000,000 square kilometers (500,000 square miles). Only 5% of the desert is sand dunes, and the area is currently expanding due to desertification (the process by which an area becomes a desert due to drought and loss of vegitation). The Gobi Desert is a cold desert, and in the winter, it reaches as low as -40 degrees Fahrenheit and as high as 122 degrees Fahrenheit in the summer.
