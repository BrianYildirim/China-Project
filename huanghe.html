Huang He

Huang He,meaning "Yellow River" in English, is one of the most popular rivers in China. It ranges from Nothern China, east-central and eastern Asia. It is called the Yellow River because of the limestone rock that surrounds the water affects the color and saturation of the river. It has a length of 3,395 miles (5,464 kilometers), as it is the country's second largest river, next to the Chang Jiang.
